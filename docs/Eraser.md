# Eraser

The **Eraser** removes whole marks — it deletes the entire highlight, stroke,
shape or comment you touch, rather than nibbling at the edges.

## How it works

- Click a mark to remove it.
- **Drag** across several marks to remove them all in one pass; the page updates
  live as each one disappears.
- A whole drag is grouped into a **single undo** step, so one `Ctrl+Z` brings
  back everything you erased in that stroke. See [[Undo and Redo]].

Erasing is a **true delete**: removed marks are dropped from the saved
`.marked.pdf` and the sidecar — they are not merely hidden. (Until you undo,
of course.)

To remove a comment from the list instead, see [[Comments Sidebar]].
